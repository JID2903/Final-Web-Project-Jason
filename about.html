About Us

At Ling's Cars, we're not your typical car leasing company—we're a bold, eccentric team driven by innovation, transparency, and a flair for the unconventional. Founded by Ling Valentine, a self-made entrepreneur known for her unique marketing style and fearless personality, our company has grown from a quirky idea into one of the most recognized online car leasing brands in the UK. Our mission is simple: make car leasing fun, affordable, and completely hassle-free. We believe in doing business with personality, and we're proud to stand out in a sea of grey-suited competitors.

From our cheeky website design to our straightforward pricing, we aim to make the car leasing process as transparent and enjoyable as possible. We offer a wide range of vehicles, from eco-friendly electric cars to luxurious SUVs, all available at competitive prices. Our online platform is designed for ease of use, allowing customers to browse, compare, and lease their dream car in just a few clicks. Plus, with our no-nonsense approach to contracts and customer service, you can trust that you're getting the best deal without any hidden surprises.

Behind the scenes, our dedicated team works tirelessly to bring customers unbeatable deals on brand-new vehicles, while delivering top-notch customer service with a side of humor. From developers and designers to leasing experts and support staff, everyone at Ling's Cars shares a passion for shaking up the industry. We're proud of our unconventional roots and even prouder of the trust we've built with thousands of happy customers across the UK. At Ling's Cars, we don't just lease cars—we turn the whole experience into something memorable.
